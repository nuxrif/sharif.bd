Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how the "Domain Info" browser extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

The Domain Info extension is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not require you to create an account, provide any personal information, or share any data that could identify you.When you use the extension, we may collect the following data, but only temporarily to fulfill your request:

  • The Domain Name: We only look up the domain name of the tab you explicitly click the extension on (e.g., example.com). We do not collect or monitor your browsing history.
  • Registrar Data: We query external services (via the RDAP protocol) to retrieve registrar information (e.g., registrar name) associated with that domain name. This data is displayed to you and is not stored by the extension.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the sole purpose of providing the extension's core functionality: displaying domain registrar information.

Specifically, we use the information to:

  • Query the official RDAP services to fetch registrar details.
  • Display the retrieved data in the extension's user interface.
  • Allow you to copy the formatted information to your clipboard.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide registrar information, Domain Info queries third-party RDAP services and registrars. These services have their own privacy policies.

When you click the extension, your browser makes a direct request to external RDAP services (like those provided by Verisign or GoDaddy) to fetch the data. This request may include your IP address, which is necessary for the communication. These services may log these requests in accordance with their own policies.

Domain Info does not use or integrate any analytics trackers, advertising networks, or other third-party scripts that track your behavior.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Domain Info is a "stateless" extension. This means we do not store any of your browsing data, the domains you look up, or the retrieved registrar information on our servers or your device's local storage after you close the extension window.

Information is retrieved in real-time and exists only within the temporary memory of the browser while the extension is active.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
