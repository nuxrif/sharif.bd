Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

Simple Truth: We value your privacy. This extension is built to be secure and private.

1. Data Collection

Multi-Point Auto Clicker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or sensitive information to any external servers.

2. Data Storage

Any settings you configure (such as click coordinates or time delays) are stored locally within your browser's storage. This data never leaves your device.

3. Permissions Usage

  • activeTab: Used only to identify click points on the current page.
  • scripting: Used to execute the click automation.
  • storage: Used to remember your point settings for future use.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties. There is no data to share.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out via our support channel.
