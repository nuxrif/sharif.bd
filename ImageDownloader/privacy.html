Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share your data. Period.

1. Information Collection

The Image Click Downloader extension does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not track your browsing history, the websites you visit, or the images you download.

2. How It Works

All processing happens locally on your computer. When you click an image, the extension simply tells your browser to download that specific URL. No data is sent to any external server.

3. Permissions

  • activeTab: To interact with images on the page you've activated.
  • downloads: To facilitate the saving of images to your local drive.

4. Third-Party Access

Since we do not collect any data, there is absolutely no data to sell or share with third parties, advertisers, or analytics companies.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, please contact the developer via the official support link on the Chrome Web Store.
